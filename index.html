Must-Visit Places in Hyderabad

Charminar

A symbol of Hyderabad, known for its rich history and vibrant market.

Golconda Fort

A majestic fort with a fascinating acoustic system and rich history.

Hussain Sagar

A beautiful lake with a giant Buddha statue in the center.

Charminar

The Charminar is an iconic monument and mosque in Hyderabad. Built in 1591 by Muhammad Quli Qutb Shah, it symbolizes the city's historical significance.
